Welcome to the SIWFederation, the home of American football enthusiasts. Established in 1986, the SIWFederation is a premier organization that brings together numerous teams from across the country.
Our mission is to foster a vibrant community where teams can compete, grow, and showcase their skills in the exciting world of American football.
At SIWFederation, we believe in the power of sports to connect people. We are committed to promoting American football and providing a platform where talent can thrive. Over the years, our teams have achieved remarkable success, setting records, winning championships, and inspiring countless fans.

“The SIWFederation is proud to have a presence across multiple states, fostering the growth and development of American football. The highlighted states on the map represent our reach and the locations of our registered teams. As we continue to grow, we aim to bring the spirit of American football to more regions.”
